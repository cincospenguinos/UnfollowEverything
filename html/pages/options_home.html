Enabled

Invisible mode (experimental)

Hide Unfollow Everything from your Facebook (it'll still run without you seeing)

Refollow mode (experimental)

Refollow friends, pages and groups that you've unfollowed. IMPORTANT: because of Facebook anti-spam controls, Refollow can only process 100 profiles a day. But if you leave Refollow mode on, eventually you'll be 100% refollowed.

You can also turn Unfollow on and off by clicking the Unfollow icon in the top right of Chrome.

If the icon isn't showing, click on the extension icon (looks like a puzzle piece) and then pin Unfollow Everything.
